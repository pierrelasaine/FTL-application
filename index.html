About me:

I am a sophomore computer science student at FIU and plan to pursue a career as a software engineer! Born and raised in Savannah, Georgia, I have always had an interest in technology from a young age. Some of my oldest memories are of quality time with my dad when he taught me about the different parts of a computer. As a distance learner, I was amazed to find that there are a number of programs that are designed with people like me in mind. Over the course of my career, I aim to contribute to programs like these and make opportunity more accessible for all.
